Flying connects people and is sometimes the fastest way to travel but it also leaves a big footprint. A flight in economy class produces about 127 CO₂/pkm while business class can reach 284 g CO₂/pkm since there’s more space per passenger.

Tip from MR. Eco: Choosing economy class or flying less often can make a big difference for the planet and your personal carbon footprint!
