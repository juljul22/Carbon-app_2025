Methodology

In Norway ferries are part of everyday life but the emissions depend on what you bring on board. A passenger ferry produces about 186 g CO₂/pkm while a car transported without passengers emits around 377 g CO₂/pkm!

Tip from MR. Eco: Whenever possible, leave the car behind and enjoy the sea breeze. Your footprint will thank you!
