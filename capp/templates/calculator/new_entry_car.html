The carbon emissions from driving depend on your vehicle's fuel type. Diesel cars produce 229 g CO₂/km, while petrol cars emit 198 g CO₂/km. Electric vehicles are the greenest option, producing just 59 g CO₂/km — roughly one-third the emissions of petrol and one-quarter of diesel vehicles.
